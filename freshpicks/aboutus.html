About Us

Meet the freshest Morning. Feel the sincerity of the farmers. We offer fresh fruits chosen by farming experts with over 30 years of know-how. It’s trustworthy.

We believe everyone deserves access to affordable, fresh, and healthy food. Starting in 1999 at the Robson Street in Vancouver, this small fruit store was able to grow with your support to become a representing business that delivers to 15 major retail stores in BC. And in 2020, we started our online business for you to meet FRESH Picks anywhere in the country.

We live in an era where we can buy things more easily and conveniently than ever before. If you can use the Internet, lie down in bed, sit on the bus and order quickly, and comfortably. From delicious BC home grown apples in the orchard of Okanagan to sweet bananas grown in Mexican soil and oranges under the California sunshine, we will bring you the best fresh fruits to your front door. I will value every customer. There are some people have never tried FRESH Picks, but none have tried it just once.
