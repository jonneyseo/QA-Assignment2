Process

Logo design: One of my classmates became a customer and I was supposed to use the colour he picked as the main colour. Then I picked four additional sub-colours that go well with the main colour. In consideration of the main targets whose ages are between 16 and 28, I created a curly logotype that looks like hand-written with brush.

Biz card: Like the logo, I came up with a 'brush' object and expressed a design like painting the top and bottom padding with a brush.

Package: The selected five colours were used with the motif of watercolour painting, and the 3D package was developed in a way that the product could stand out well without distorting the colour by properly using the global light and spotlight. On the side, an embossing seal (bumped logo) were applied to certify the product's genuineness.
