Ciao Italia

It is a 10-page magazine production project to be familiar with manipulating Adobe InDesign. I have created the one that contains travel information named 'Ciao Italia'. As a person who values 'the beauty of the margins' in layout design, putting many articles and photos in a given space was the challenge. As a travel lover, I tried to use every photograph used in this project to be what I previously took during travel.

Process

Photo correction: It was not easy to use photographs that were not intended for magazines, as they were not taken in consideration of the space in which the articles would be placed. In addition, it took long time to retouch 23 different photos to a consistent tone and manner.

Collecting articles: I used articles written by experts such as the Lonely Planet blog. However, the process of unifying the writing style and nuance of the words was necessary as various sources were cited.

Layout design: It was also important to style the layout, as well as maintain a consistent style so that there was no different looking across pages.
