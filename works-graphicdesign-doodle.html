Doodle

A fictional advertising company named 'Doodle', the purpose of this project was to design stationery that builds its brand recognition. By this project, I was able to learn how to apply core design principles into a real-world problem. (e.g. Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity)

Process

Baseline: Using the 'creative' properties of the advertising company and the sweet and stylish context of the word 'Doodle', the oblique and zigzag lines were chosen as motifs so they reflected in the logo.

Colour Theme: I intended to use a strong colour that could imprint the company's presence on customers. The high-saturated navy and orange colour were followed by sub colours to harmonize with these two.

Based on the motif and colour, each asset was designed accordingly. In particular, the envelope was inspired by the airmail envelope pattern from the past, which are rarely seen these days.
