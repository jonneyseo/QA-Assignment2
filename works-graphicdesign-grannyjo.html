Granny Jo

The design package development of a 65-year-old vintage Apple Cider brand. (Fictitious) This project was to create the custom logo on a 3D beverage container in a marketing poster. The focus was using 3D effects such as Revolve, Bevel & Extrude to draw out the object based on real life.

Process

Logo-making: To express the attribute of the brand well, I intended to make a vintage-looking logo that contains history. A grandmotherâ€™s sewing with a warm-hearted mind gave me a motif. In this regard, it is expressed by adding lines that are repeated in thin thickness within a basic drawing.

3D Container: 3D was one of the requirements for this project, but in fact, Illustrator was not a professional 3D tool, so there was room for it to be expressed in a rustic and unnatural manner. To prevent this, several mock-ups were created according to the strength of the effect and the light control, and the most suitable one was selected. Along with this, 3D apples were created.

Poster: To put in a picture that blends with the vintage concept, the photos of the old lady and the tree were photoshopped. Also, a grainy background image was used as a vector mask to convey old paper texture.
