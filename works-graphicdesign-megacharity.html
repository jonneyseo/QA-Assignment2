M.E.G.A. Charity

M.E.G.A. (Make the Earth Green Again) is a fictitious charity fights against global warming. This project was to produce three series of posters for various promotional purposes: 1) ‘HELP’ to promote donations to the public, 2) ‘KNOW’ to promote a conference and 3) ‘ACT’ to inform people of their practices in daily life.

Process

Concept planning: Poster design should have eye-catching elements and compelling messages to the point of attention from afar. So I decided to organize it in order from top to bottom, giving ‘Fact’, throwing ‘Message’, and explaining detailed ‘Information’. Also, yellow, green, and red colours were selected as theme colours for each series. It was the intention to draw attention like a traffic light from a distance.

Photo selection and retouch: Each poster has backgrounded photos that with the content. So I considered the harmony with the text, then adjusted the intensity, saturation, vibration, highlight, and transparency. The picture in the last series 'ACT' was taken by myself.

Style consistency: The three completed posters had put in a common element so that they can have similar looking. The layout, font, icons, logos, and QR codes had been placed.
